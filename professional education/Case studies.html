Clinical Case Studies

Gain insights from real world dental cases showcasing diagnosis, treatment planning, and evidence based clinical decision making.

Restorative Dentistry: Class II Restoration

A 24-year-old patient presented with proximal caries on tooth #26. Composite restoration technique was used for optimal aesthetics and function.

Orthodontic Correction: Class I Malocclusion

Comprehensive case showing correction with clear aligners and fixed retainers, focusing on facial symmetry and long term stability.

Endodontic Retreatment

A failed root canal was retreated using rotary instrumentation and apex locator guidance. The case emphasizes accurate canal debridement.

Pediatric Dentistry: Space Maintainer

Managing early loss of primary molars using customized space maintainers to preserve arch integrity and prevent misalignment.

Periodontal Therapy

Treatment of chronic gingivitis with scaling, root planing, and patient education on oral hygiene reinforcement.

Prosthodontics: Full Mouth Rehabilitation

A 58-year-old patient underwent complete oral rehabilitation using fixed partial dentures and implant-supported restorations.
