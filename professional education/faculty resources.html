Faculty Resources

Access comprehensive resources designed to enhance your teaching, advance your research, and support effective academic planning.

Teaching Guidelines

Delivering quality dental education requires both clinical expertise and effective teaching strategies. This section provides a comprehensive framework for academic excellence, classroom engagement, and professional ethics in dental education.

  • Faculty Roles & Responsibilities: Establish clear academic and clinical expectations to ensure consistency in teaching and supervision.
  • Interactive Teaching Methodologies: Apply evidence-based instructional techniques that enhance student participation and understanding.
  • Ethical Practices: Uphold academic integrity and promote a culture of respect, fairness, and professionalism in all learning environments.
  • Classroom Management: Foster a positive and inclusive environment that encourages discipline, collaboration, and student confidence.

Course Planning & Syllabus Templates

Effective course design is fundamental to achieving desired learning outcomes. This section offers structured tools and templates to assist faculty in developing cohesive and outcome-oriented academic plans.

  • Syllabus Templates: Ready-to-use formats for course outline creation.
  • Learning outcome matrix: Framework to align objectives, teaching methods, and assessments.
  • Weekly lecture planners: Structured layouts for lesson sequencing and time allocation.
  • Practical session structure: Guidelines for laboratory and clinical session organization.

Evaluation & Assessment Tools

Fair and objective assessment plays a vital role in maintaining educational standards. This section includes customizable tools and templates for evaluating student knowledge, skills, and professionalism.

  • Skill Assessment Rubrics: Standardized criteria for practical and clinical evaluations.
  • Exam Templates: Ready formats for MCQ, short-answer, and case-based assessments
  • Peer & Self-Evaluation Forms: Instruments that encourage reflection and peer feedback.
  • Attendance & Participation Trackers: Simplified sheets for monitoring student engagement.

Research & Development

Research forms the backbone of innovation in dental education and practice. This section provides resources to support faculty in research planning, collaboration, and publication.

  • Research Proposal Templates: Standard formats for developing and submitting research ideas.
  • Access to Journals: Direct links to peer-reviewed dental and biomedical publications.
  • Ethical Research Guidelines: Documentation outlining institutional and international standards.
  • Publication Assistance: Contacts and resources for manuscript preparation and journal submission.

Faculty Training Workshops

Continuous professional development ensures that educators stay updated with modern teaching practices and emerging clinical trends. This section highlights upcoming training sessions and provides access to past workshop materials.

  • Faculty Development Programs: Regular sessions on pedagogy and clinical innovations.
  • Workshop Archives: Access to recordings and reference materials from previous events.
  • Certificates of Participation: Recognition for completed training sessions.
  • Newsletter Subscription: Stay informed about upcoming workshops and institutional updates.
